Pente






Rules

  • The winner is the first player to place 5 pieces in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) or make 5 captures. If the board is full, the game ends in a draw.
  • A capture occurs if a Player A surrounds or blocks in 2 pieces from Player B.
  • If a player moves into a capture (i.e. their move results in themselves being captured), that capture does not count for the opposing player.
